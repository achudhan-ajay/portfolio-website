What i’m into

Beyond the pixels and the prototypes, my design is fuelled by a constant stream of high-fidelity inputs. Whether I’m analysing the deep metaphors of an Art Film, exploring the immersive mechanics of AAA and Indie games, or getting lost in the rhythm of a curated playlist, I’m always looking for that next spark of inspiration.

When I’m not behind a screen, you’ll find me at the intersection of discipline and curiosity — training for marathons, hitting the badminton court, or diving into a book on human behaviour. For me, life is an endless loop of learning random things and finding the art in every detail.
